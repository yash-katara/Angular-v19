Directives In Angular 19

Directives are used to change the DOM structure or behavior.

There are three types of directives in Angular 19

  • Components
  • Structural Directives
  • Attribute Directives

Components are the directives with a template.

Structural directives are used to change the DOM structure.

Attribute directives are used to change the behavior of the DOM elements.

Structural directives are used to add or remove elements from the DOM.

Attribute directives are used to change the behavior of the DOM elements.

*ngFor Directives

*ngFor is a structural directive that is used to iterate over a collection and render a template for each item in the collection.

It is used to display a list of items in the DOM.
